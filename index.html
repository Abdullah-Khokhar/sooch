our mission

Our mission is to help many white collar families as we can. To reach out to people who don't reach out for any kind or help. To help those who are actually needy and making efforts to fulfil their needs. We will be providing everything we can including blood donors, education/ fee donors, medical case solutions and much more.

Make donations

Make Donations is not a limit with financial help only. It includes blood donors too. Providing a shelter, providing any employment so that they can work on their own too.

Help & support

We'll be having proper and easy options, for every common person to use. They can ask for any medical help, any educational help, any blood related queries. We will try our best to educate them about sooch.

our campaigns

We are working on different campaigns These are : Free food drives, Aik Khana rozana, Medical camps, Sasti roti sasta salan, Winter drives, Orphanage visits, Old age home visits, School visits, ramzan ration drives, winter drives, Eid drives etc

Our Causes

DASTAR KHAWAN

Dastarkhwan is basically to provide food for everyone. The amount is collected and we arrange food which is affordable for everyone. Anyone can come and eat. Aftar drives also provides a dastarkhwan for everyone. Around 150-200 people covered in a single dastarkhwan.

EDUCATION

We believe that education is the most important part that must be provided to everyone. We try to find children who wants to study but cannot because of financial issues.We are providing free education to some of the Children. We have provided them their uniforms, arranged books for them too.

MEDICAL CASES

Medical cases requires a lot of amount. We have set the limit for the cases because it is not easy to collect donations in Lacs for a single case. We do help as much as we can but we need to keep the amount limit. Sometimes medical references help in free medication or in medical tests.

CHARITY

As it is said, Charity starts from home. Charity never depends on only financial help. Even smile is also considered as charity. We believe in every kind of help , Every good gesture is charity.

About us

The main objective of a website will, to reach out Maximum deserving white collar families in less time. To provide them a platform that have every type of help they would need. They won’t have to wait for the calls and confirmations in blood related urgent situations. The website will have proper person dealing as a councilor too.
